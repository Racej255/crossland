Grand Crossland Gala

Join us to celebrate Marcus Crossland’s birthday at his annual gala.

Learn More

Event Details

Step into a world of grandeur as House Crossland welcomes you to the Grand Crossland Gala. Set within the historic Priest’s House, this is more than just a celebration—it’s a banquet of tradition, revelry and spectacle. Raise a goblet of mulled wine, feast in a hall rich with history and dance beneath the flickering glow of candlelight.

Whether you are a returning guest or experiencing the gala for the first time, this is an evening you won’t want to miss.

Date: 17 January 2026

Time: 17:00 – 23:30

Location: The Priest’s House, Barden Tower, Skipton BD23 6AS

Example Schedule

  • 17:00 – Arrival: Mulled wine and warm welcomes.
  • 17:30 – Time‑in Begins: Guests start settling in.
  • 18:00 – Banquet Hall Opens: Step inside for a feast worthy of legend.
  • 20:15 – Entertainment Begins: Prepare to be enthralled.
  • 21:00 – Dancing & Celebration: Join the ceilidh or watch the revelry unfold.
  • 23:00 – Time‑in Ends: The night winds down.

Note: Schedule is an example from 2025; the 2026 event may differ slightly.

Example Menu Options

  • Mixed vegetable broth
  • Poacher’s salmon with wild dressed leaves
  • Slow braised lamb shank, rosemary and red wine jus
  • King’s meat stew (rich stew of venison, pheasant, rabbit and beef)
  • Tartlet of apple and ginger with cinder ice cream
  • Cheeseboards
  • Mixed vegetable broth
  • Almond fritter, chickpea & saffron dip
  • Mixed nut tartlet with peas, parsley and mint sauce
  • Butternut squash & mushroom Wellington
  • Poached pears in spiced red wine
  • Cheeseboards

Menus are examples from 2025; the 2026 event menu may vary.

Costs & Payment

Total Price: £95 per guest

Deposit: £45 to secure your position

Remaining Balance: £50 due no later than 1 December (can be paid earlier)

This price includes unlimited beer, wine, cider and soft drinks. Guests will enjoy a night for the senses with sumptuous food, roaring entertainment and memorable souvenirs.

Spaces: Limited to 40 guests
