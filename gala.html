Grand Crossland Gala

Join us to celebrate Marcus Crossland’s birthday at his annual gala.

Learn More

Event Details

Step into a world of grandeur as House Crossland welcomes you to the Grand Crossland Gala. Set within the historic Priest’s House, this is more than just a celebration - it’s a banquet of grandeur, revelry and spectacle. Raise a goblet of mulled wine, feast in a hall rich with history and dance beneath the flickering glow of candlelight.

Whether you are a returning guest or experiencing the gala for the first time, this is an evening you won’t want to miss.

Date: 16 January 2027

Time: 17:00 – 23:30

Location: The Priest’s House, Barden Tower, Skipton BD23 6AS

Example Schedule

  • 17:00 – Arrival: Mulled wine and warm welcomes.

  • 17:30 – Time‑in Begins.

  • 18:00 – Banquet Hall Opens: Step inside for a magnificant feast.

  • 20:15 – Entertainment Begins: Prepare to be enthralled.

  • 21:00 – Dancing & Celebrations: Join the ceilidh or watch the revelry unfold.

  • 23:00 – Time‑in Ends: The night winds down.

  • 23:15 - Carriages await: Guests must vacate the venue (But there's an OC afterparty on the same site!)

Note: Schedule is an example from 2026; the 2027 event may differ slightly.

Costs & Payment

Payment: Full payment is required to secure your spot.
